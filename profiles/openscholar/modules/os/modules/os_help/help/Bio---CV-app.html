The Bio/CV app lets you post and manage your Curriculum Vitae and Biography. This app is a vital part of a personal scholar site. Visitors of your site will want to know information about you and your academic background, achievements, goals, projects, ideas, etc.

To enable the Bio/CV app:

  1. Go to Control Panel > Build > Apps
  2. Set the Bio/CV App status to "Public" or "Private".
  3. Save your app configuration.

Bio/CV Settings

Once the Bio/CV app is enabled the following settings are available:

  • Curriculum Vitae Display: Choose whether to display a separate sub-page for your CV, with it's own configurable text and optional PDF attachment, or to upload a PDF, hide the separate CV page, and display a PDF download link on your Bio page.
  • Comment Settings: Whether to allow comments to be posted to your public Bio and CV posts.

Viewing your Bio and CV

Enabling this app will create a "Bio" link in the primary menu of your site which will display your Bio and CV. After clicking on this link, you will see two tabs called "Biography" and "Curriculum Vitae". Click on the appropriate tab to toggle between your Bio or CV.
